# Motors

Read more about types of motors at [Adafruit's Motors Guide.](https://learn.adafruit.com/adafruit-motor-selection-guide)

----
Motors available at hackSpace

- [DC MOTORs](https://github.com/kingston-hackSpace/Motors/blob/main/DCmotors.md)

  - High Speed, one direction (left or right, depending on wiring).
  - Continuous movement (doesn't hold position).
  - Good for smooth, continuous motion.
 
- [SERVO MOTORs](https://github.com/kingston-hackSpace/Motors/blob/main/SERVOmotors.md)

  - Rotate at specific angles (180 or 360 degrees).
  - Precise, small and light.
  - Can hold position while powered.

- [STEPPER MOTORs](https://github.com/kingston-hackSpace/Motors/blob/main/STEPPERmotors.md)

  - Precise motion (by steps). Fully programmable: speed, direction, and position.
  - Can hold position while powered.
 
- SOLENOID MOTOR (5V)

- VIBRATION MOTOR (5V)
